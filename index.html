    A little background;

    Though I attend Laguardia High school as a vocal major, I enjoy art in many mediums and aspire to have some sort of art career.
    My art is "social surrealism" (my coined term) ; As I am constantly intrigued by the concept of our societal structure.
    My pieces often have heavy contrast and images full of expression with animalistic traits and overstated concepts.
